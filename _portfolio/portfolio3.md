  **Description**
  <p style="text-align: justify;">
  A workshop in the field of congestion management, combining international expert lectures, structured discussions, and poster presentations. As grid congestion becomes a critical issue, congestion management stands out as the most efficient solution. This workshop gathers researchers and professionals in power systems, control, and related fields to exchange insights. Through expert lectures, structured discussions, and poster sessions, we aim to foster multidisciplinary collaboration, share the latest developments, and connect young researchers with the field's pioneers.
  </p>
  Sponsored by EE-NL and Eindhoven University Of Technology, EES group, having 3 invited guest speakers.

  **Role**
  Co-organizer together with Xin Li and Nikolaos G. Paterakis
